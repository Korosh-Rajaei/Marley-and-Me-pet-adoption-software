Baxter
Meet Baxter, a medium-sized, charmingly scruffy dog with a heart of gold! Baxter has a soft, wavy coat that’s a mix of warm caramel and creamy white, giving him an irresistibly huggable look. His ears are floppy and perk up when he's curious, which is often, as he's a naturally inquisitive dog who loves exploring his surroundings.

Baxter’s eyes are a deep, soulful brown, always full of warmth and a hint of mischief. He's the type of dog who'll greet you at the door with a wagging tail, bringing you his favorite toy as a welcome gift. Friendly with everyone, he gets along well with other pets and loves playing with kids.

Loyal, playful, and gentle, Baxter's the perfect mix of energy and calmness. Whether it's an outdoor adventure or a cozy nap by your side, he's happiest when he's with his family.
